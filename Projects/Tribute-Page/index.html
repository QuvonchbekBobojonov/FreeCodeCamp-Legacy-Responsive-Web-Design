G-Eazy

G-Eazy performing on stage during a live concert.

Gerald Earl Gillum, professionally known as G-Eazy, is an American rapper and songwriter. He rose to international popularity after releasing his album These Things Happen in 2014. His music combines introspective lyrics with modern hip-hop production and storytelling.

Known for hit songs like "Me, Myself & I" and "No Limit", G-Eazy quickly became a global name in the music industry. Over his career, he has released multiple platinum albums and worked with many top artists.
